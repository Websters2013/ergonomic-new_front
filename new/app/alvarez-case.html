The Project

AlvarezCapital find properties to invest in, refresh and sell or rent them with a plus value.

How do they do it?

They share the risk of the investment with private investors by giving them 5 to 8% interest rate on a 30% gobal return investment. A casual project is taking 60 months.

Each month the investor receives interests and at the end of the period (60months) AlvarezCapital refund the initial investment.
